Call philosophy

Humane Does Not Mean Weak

Many debtors are not used to being treated humanely by a collector. This toolkit teaches collectors how to stay respectful without becoming passive, direct without becoming hostile, and structured without sounding robotic.

How the flow works

A repeatable path from contact to documented outcome.

  1. 1Verify/contact according to company policy
  2. 2Avoid disconnect
  3. 3State the amount due
  4. 4Ask: "Can you pay that today?"
  5. 5If no: "How much can you pay today?"
  6. 6If nothing today: "When can you make a payment?"
  7. 7Use APT if no clear commitment
  8. 8Handle objections and emotional cues
  9. 9Close, document, and route
