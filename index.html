Call philosophy

Many debtors are not used to being treated humanely by a collector. This toolkit teaches collectors how to stay respectful without becoming passive, direct without becoming hostile, and structured without sounding robotic.

The Collector Productivity Call Flow Toolkit can also be delivered as a live training and pilot program for collection agencies, departments, and teams that want to test the system in a controlled, manager-reviewed setting.
